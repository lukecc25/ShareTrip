ShareTrip Guide

How It Works

ShareTrip helps drivers and passengers connect for shared rides. The website provides the ride board and coordination tools, but each trip still depends on clear communication and good judgment between people.

For Drivers

Create a Ride Offer

  1. Go to the ride board and choose to create a ride.
  2. Enter your starting location, destination, travel date, and departure time.
  3. Add the number of available seats and any helpful details passengers should know.
  4. Review passenger requests and communicate clearly before confirming plans.
For Passengers

Create a Ride Request

  1. Go to the ride board and choose to create a ride request.
  2. Share where you need to be picked up, where you are going, and when you hope to travel.
  3. Include the number of passengers and any important timing or luggage details.
  4. Watch for driver offers and confirm the final plan directly with the driver.

Money and Trust

Payment Is Handled Outside ShareTrip

ShareTrip does not collect money, process payments, hold deposits, or manage reimbursements. Drivers and passengers are responsible for making their own arrangements for gas money, shared costs, or any other payment details.

Because those arrangements happen between people, every trip is based on trust. Agree on expectations before the ride begins, including cost, pickup location, timing, route, and contact information.

Stay Cautious

Connect Carefully

Verify Details

Confirm the driver's name, passenger names, destination, pickup spot, departure time, and cost arrangement before meeting.

Meet Wisely

Use public, well-lit locations when possible and let someone you trust know your travel plans.

Trust Your Judgment

If something feels unclear or uncomfortable, pause the plan, ask more questions, or choose not to ride.
